The Power of Good Typography

Good typography is more than just choosing a pretty font. It’s about creating a visual hierarchy, guiding the reader’s eye, and making the text effortless to consume. Fonts like Playfair Display are elegant and ideal for headings because they draw attention and add a sense of sophistication. For body text, Roboto is a popular choice because it is clean, modern, and easy to read on all screen sizes.

When designing a website, picking the right fonts can significantly impact user experience. A well-chosen heading font gives personality to your content, while a simple and legible paragraph font ensures readers stay engaged. The balance between beauty and readability is what makes great typography powerful.
